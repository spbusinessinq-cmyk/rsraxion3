Improve source reliability scoring.

official government, regulatory, institutional, primary-source feeds

TIER 2:
major wire services and established news organizations

TIER 3:
specialist analysis outlets

TIER 4:
unknown / weak / inconsistent sources

Use source tier in confidence scoring.

- domain consistency
